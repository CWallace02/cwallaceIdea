Secretary Of State On Wheels

On the Secretary of State Michigan.gov’s website there are online services, but homebound individuals may need assistance completing those services and who better to help than a SOS Professional. The in office services and self services will be brought to and from your doorstep as well for homebound senior citizens.
Sometimes services aren’t available through the SOS website and you have to make an appointment for an office visit. Many of us are aware of how hectic it can be at times to schedule an appointment, wait in long lines, and sit in the waiting room for long hours. SOS on Wheels gives senior citizens the opportunity to skip those long waits and hecticness in the comfort of their homes.
During the COVID-19 Pandemic older adults are at greater risk to severe illness according to cdc.gov. This is due to old age or if they already have underlying medical conditions. If unvaccinated they are most likely to be hospitalized or die from COVID-19. SOS on wheels is a preventive measure because they will not have to be a part of a large crowd around unvaccinated others to transmit the virus. We have COVID-19 precautions set in place for the employees/volunteers who are going from house to house in order to not transmit or receive the virus (vaccine required positions).
